How to become part of SMUHI

This a simple 3 step guide on how to apply and gain admission into Saint Monica University Higher Institute

Open an admission account

Admission into the University happens all year round. You do not yet have an admission account? start by creating one here New Application Account, if you already have an admission account, login using the link My admission account. Fill the forms and obtain and account. When you are logged in, begin a new application or resume an application you started earlier or see status of submitted applications for programs you applied for already.

Apply for 1 or more programs

When you are logged into your admissions account area, click on the New Application button to begin a new application. In the application process, you will be asked the programs/departments you want to apply in, at this step you will only be allowed to apply for multiple programs within the same school/faculty you selected in the previous form entry in one application. Which means based on this single application, you might be accepted to study in one program only amongst the choices you applied for. This increase your chances of been accepted in a particular school. If you intend to apply to more programs/departments found under different schools/faculties, you do this by starting a new application but this time around selecting the school/faculty you wish to apply in and the programs/departments under that school/faculty/

Get admitted into a program

At the end of the admission process, submit your application after verfiying that all information is accurately entered. After submission, you will be notified that your application has been received successfully and how long you should wait to see a response on your candidacy on the programs you applied for. You also have a choice to subscribe for notification by SMS and/or email when your application status changes. Once your status changes, you shall receive a message accompanied by a letter with instructions on what steps to take next.

FAQ - We are here to help!

How do I apply?

Apply by creating an admission account using the link New Admission Account. Follow the instructions on the form, create and account. With this new account, login using the link My Admission Account. Once logged in, start a new application using the New Application button. Follow the form instructions to apply for a program

How do I know if I have been accepted or not?

There are many ways to know your application status:
* Check your email used for the admission account frequently for an email from the admissions office concerning your admission status.
* Login into your admission account and on the table of applications you will see the status of each application under the status colum of the table.
* If you subscribed for SMS notification, you will receive an SMS notification on your application status on the phone number your provided when you were applying.
